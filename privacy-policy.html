Privacy Policy for Adam Freides Marketing Reporting

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Data Collection and Usage

Adam Freides Marketing Reporting uses the Google Ads API to fetch campaign performance metrics and reporting data. This application accesses data solely to generate marketing reports.

2. Data Storage and Sharing

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data or Google Ads account data to third-party servers. All data retrieved via the Google Ads API is processed locally and is used exclusively for internal analytical reporting. We do not sell or share any data.

3. Security

We implement standard technical safeguards to protect data transmission, utilizing secure OAuth 2.0 protocols provided by Google for authentication.
